Kanji

This Kanji meaning memorize is made up of the Radicals viking and see. Imagine a viking having to naviagte his ship through oceans. Being granted his ability to see he is able to recognize areas he once memorized.

Radicals

There is a roof with extra spikes/horns on it. Who was famous for having spiked roofs, spiked helmets, spiked boats?! The vikings! That's why this is the viking radical.

This radical means to see. There's an eyeball on top of some legs. When your eyes get taken places (on legs, in this case), your eyes are seeing those things. Think about the Lord of Rings. The Eye of Mordor pretty much stands on something like legs and sees everything, well almost everything.
